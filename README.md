# ArenaX: The Living Game 

**ArenaX** is an elite second screen sports platform that transforms passive viewing into an active, high intensity experience. It bridges the gap between the broadcast and the fan with a "Living Interface" powered by generative AI and real time social energy.

---

##  Quick Start: Experience the Arena
1. **Setup**: `npm install`
2. **Launch**: `npm run dev`
3. **View**: Open `http://localhost:5173/`
4. **Demo Trick**: Click the **Settings (⚙️)** and turn on **Replay Mode** to see a full match lifecycle in 60 seconds.

---

##  The Project Gyst (Concise)
ArenaX solves the "Passive Fan" problem by providing four layers of deep engagement:

### 1. Intelligence Layer (AI Co-Pilot)
- **Generative Commentary**: Powered by Google Gemini patterns. Choose between **Tactical**, **Hype**, or **Nerd** personas that react live to the game flow.
- **Adaptive Insights**: AI analyzes match momentum and xG (Expected Goals) to provide "Ambient" tactical updates every few seconds.

### 2. Immersive Layer (Sensory UX)
- **Tactical Radar**: Real-time 3D player tracking for Football (Pitch) and Cricket (Field Radar).
- **Haptic Storytelling**: Physical vibrations for goals/boundaries and **3D POV Seating** (North Stand, VIP Box) to change your vantage point.
- **Dynamic Waveforms**: Real time momentum and emotional intensity tracking.

### 3. Social Layer (Fan Sync)
- **Faction Battles**: Real time "Home vs Away" tug-of-war fueled by live fan cheers.
- **Crowd Heatmaps**: Glowing hotspots on the field that show where thousands of fans are interacting.
- **Watch Party Rooms**: Private sync for friends with shared prediction leaderboards.

### 4. Data Layer (Elite Analytics)
- **Win Probability River**: A flowing visual of match control volatility.
- **Historical Parallel Overlay**: Faded "Ghost Scores" comparing the live match to legendary historical games.
- **Fan Report Card**: A post match AI analysis of your "Aura" and prediction accuracy.
